The Hold a Hand Project

The Hold a Hand Project was instituted to provide financial support to underprivileged Nigerian students. As college students ourselves, we believe that education can change the course of a young personâ€™s life. Our aim is to empower promising Nigerian students to continue to strive for excellence even in the midst of tremendous adversity. To achieve this, we have established relationships with Nigerian universities to facilitate our application/award processes and ensure applicant credibility. We hope that you find it in your heart to support our cause by making a donation.

Donate

We are 100% funded by charitable donations. Your donations help us increase the number of students who receive financial aid from the program. Relying on your assistance, we look forward to increasing diversity in our recipients as well expanding to accommodate more public Nigerian universities. We encourage you to consider making monthly donations as an investment for this purpose. You can suspend your donations at any time. Thank you.

Selection Process

We work directly with college professors in different schools in Nigeria to identify high achieving students who are struggling to stay in school due to financial constraints. Following a competitive application process, eligible candidates undergo in-person interviews with professors assisting the Hold a Hand project in these schools; then, selected candidates are awarded tuition scholarships to assist them in furthering their education. More information (contact information, education history, etc.) about any recipient can be obtained on request by any donor. The number of students we sponsor solely depends on the availability of funds.
